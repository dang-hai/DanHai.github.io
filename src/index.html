I'm a third year PhD student at University of Bayreuth conducting research on human-AI collaboration for creative tasks. I'm part of the HCI+AI group with Dr. Daniel Buschek. Previously, I received an M.Sc in Computer Science and a B.Sc. in Human-Computer-Interaction from the LMU Munich. During my undergraduate studies, I focused on user interaction research and machine learning to analyze and model human behavior.

Now, I apply user research methods to understand how users interact with AI to create digital artifacts. As part of my research, I build interactive systems to support people explore and control the generative capabilities of new AI systems. My research is funded by the Bavarian State Ministry of Science and the Arts and coordinated by the Bavarian Research Institute for Digital Transformation (bidt).
